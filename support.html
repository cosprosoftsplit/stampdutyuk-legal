Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate stamp duty?

Enter the property purchase price and select your buyer status (first-time buyer, home mover, or additional property). The App calculates your estimated Stamp Duty Land Tax.

Are the rates up to date?

Yes. We update stamp duty rates and thresholds whenever HMRC announces changes.

Does it cover first-time buyer relief?

Yes. Select 'First-time buyer' to see the reduced rates and thresholds that apply to eligible buyers.

Does it include the additional property surcharge?

Yes. Select 'Additional property' to include the 3% surcharge on second homes and buy-to-let properties.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Does this replace professional advice?

No. The App provides estimates only. For complex property transactions, consult a solicitor or conveyancer.

The App is not working properly. What should I do?

If the issue persists, please contact us at

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the App installed
  • Restart the App
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the App
